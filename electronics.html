The electronic system architecture of the vehicle has been designed allowing the software systems to achieve optimal control of the vehicle with ease and robustness. Besides scalability in future, this architecture emphasizes on prominent work division while ensuring efficient power distribution. Majority of the boards are designed and populated in-house to achieve the mentioned objectives.
The Electronic system is two layer modular architecture.The single board computer does the higher level processing and the Beaglebone Black does the interfacing with the electronic system.
